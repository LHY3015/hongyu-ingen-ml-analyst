 For the PIC 2.0 model classes(AMDC, STUM, HTD-IRL, GRPO, SEOM, CRL-MRS), what I most interested in and familiar with are **GRPO** and **CRL-MRS**. When I was at the early stage doing my drone path planning thesis, I did literature review and chose among many planning methods. RL is one of the directions that I felt intrigued because it can be applied in both planning and control part. Its motivation and core idea are intuitive, while the formulation is elegant. Besides, Swarm Cooperative UAV Formation is also covered in the review, and the dual robot collaborative system take muti agent ralation into condideration as well. I believe that the multi-agent collaboration (corresponding to CRL-MRS)  is a highly promising direction, whether for virtual AI agents or physical AI systems.
